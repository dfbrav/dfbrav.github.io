Patawarta Drone Datasets  

4 Flights were flown. The northernmost survey ran into connectivity issues but focused on a hard to reach portion of the rim dolomite. The survey just south of it captures a significant part of the rim dolomite and an area of measured Wonoka used to constrain relative ages. The third survey is to the SE and covers the area and data collected for exploring the Wonoka inclusions previously interpreted in this area. The fourth and easternmost survey captures most of the area Sarah and I sampled for to test isotope stratigraphy within halokinetic sequences.

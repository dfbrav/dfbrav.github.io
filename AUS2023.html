I'll load my drone data from Patawarta on this page for easy download from the team. I'm happy to add your data if you would like me to.

Patawarta Drone Datasets  

4 Flights were flone. A northern survey capturing a part of the Rim Dolomite, a halokinetic sequence survey covering part of the area Sarah and I sampled for isotope studies along an HKS, a salt sheet inclusion survey to be used in the paper addressing wonoka inclusions in Patawarta, and a remote survey targeting a hard to reach portion of the rim dolomite. Unfortunately, I was not able to link everything up, and that work is left for the future. A map is shown below showing the survey distributions. The basemaps will be shared on a onedrive link below, and each model will be loaded into sketchfab and hosted here for inspection.
